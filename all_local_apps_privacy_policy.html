Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

Welcome to All Local Apps and its community-focused applications.

All Local Apps provides digital platforms that help local communities access useful information, announcements, listings, services, and resources.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use any application developed under the All Local Apps platform.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

All Local Apps applications do not require users to create an account, sign up, or log in.

We do not collect or store personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Profile information
  • Login credentials
  • Personal account information

Users can access community information without providing personal details.

2. Community Information and Listings

Our applications may display community-related information including announcements, listings, images, services, events, and other resources.

This information may be provided by community administrators, organizations, or authorized contributors to help serve local residents.

We do not sell, rent, or use community information for advertising purposes.

3. Listing Reports and Feedback

Some applications may provide options to report incorrect, inappropriate, or outdated listings.

Information submitted through reporting or feedback features is used only for reviewing content quality, improving services, and maintaining a useful community platform.

4. Notifications

Our applications may use push notifications to provide important community updates, announcements, and service-related information.

Notifications are used only to improve communication between communities and users. Users can control notification permissions through their device settings.

5. External Communication Services

Some community listings or information pages may include links or contact options for external communication services such as WhatsApp or other messaging platforms.

When you choose to contact someone using an external service, your interaction takes place directly with that third-party service provider.

All Local Apps does not access, collect, store, or manage your messages, contacts, phone numbers, or conversations on external communication platforms.

Your use of external services is governed by the privacy policies and terms of those respective service providers.

6. Third-Party Services

To operate and maintain our applications, we may use trusted technology service providers such as cloud hosting and application infrastructure providers.

These services may process limited technical information required for application operation, security, reliability, and performance.

We do not use third-party services to create personal user profiles or track users for advertising purposes.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information used by our applications and maintain a secure service.

However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

All Local Apps applications are designed for community information access and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Since our applications do not require user accounts or personal information, children can use the applications without providing personal details.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our applications, services, or applicable requirements.

Any updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
