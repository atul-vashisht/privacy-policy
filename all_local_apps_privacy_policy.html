Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2026

Welcome to All Local Apps. This application is designed to help local communities access useful information, updates, listings, and resources related to their community.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

All Local Apps does not require user registration or login. We do not collect, store, or maintain any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • Profile information
  • Personal account details

Users can access the application without creating an account or providing personal information.

2. Community Content

The application displays local community information, listings, announcements, images, and other content intended to help community members stay informed.

Some content may be provided by community administrators or authorized contributors. We do not claim ownership of user-submitted or community-provided content.

3. Listing Reports

The application may provide an option to report incorrect, inappropriate, or problematic listings.

When submitting a report, users may provide information necessary to describe the issue. We use this information only for reviewing and improving the quality of community content.

4. Notifications

The application may use push notifications to provide important community updates, announcements, or service-related information.

Notifications are used only to improve communication between the community and application users. Users can manage notification permissions through their device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services such as cloud hosting and application infrastructure providers to operate and maintain the application.

These services may process limited technical information required for application functionality. We do not use these services to collect personal user profiles or track users for advertising purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect application data and maintain a secure service. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

All Local Apps does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the application does not require user accounts or personal information, children can use the application without providing personal details.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the application or legal requirements. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
